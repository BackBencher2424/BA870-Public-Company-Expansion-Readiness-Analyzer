# BA870-Public-Company-Expansion-Readiness-Analyzer

#### **Members:**  Vishesh Goyal, Nathan Leung, Sarah Dsouza

## What is our problem Statement
A beginner-friendly financial analytics project that compares a selected public company with peer firms in the same industry and estimates whether the company looks financially ready to expand. We use historical WRDS Compustat annual data to train the model locally in Google Colab, and then we use a lightweight Streamlit app for presentation and scoring.

## Why we chose this project?  
To help the companies to be financially prepared for a healthy future growth when compared to its peers
